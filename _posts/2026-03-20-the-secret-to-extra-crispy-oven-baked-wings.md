# The Secret to Extra Crispy Oven-Baked Wings

Everyone loves a plate of crispy, juicy chicken wings, but nobody loves the mess and grease of deep-frying at home. If you've been searching for that perfect crunch without the vat of oil, look no further. The secret to restaurant-quality wings is likely already in your pantry: **baking powder**.

## Why This Method Works

Baking powder (ensure it is aluminum-free!) changes the pH level of the chicken skin, allowing it to break down and crisp up more effectively. When combined with a high-heat oven, the moisture evaporates rapidly, leaving you with skin that is shatteringly crisp while the meat inside remains tender and succulent.

## Ingredients

*   2 lbs chicken wings (flats and drums separated)
*   1 tablespoon aluminum-free baking powder
*   1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
*   1/2 teaspoon black pepper
*   1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
*   Your favorite buffalo or BBQ sauce for tossing

## Instructions

1.  **Dry the Wings:** Use paper towels to pat the wings as dry as possible. Any surface moisture will create steam and prevent the skin from getting truly crispy.
2.  **The Coating:** In a large bowl, toss the wings with the baking powder, salt, pepper, and garlic powder until every wing is lightly and evenly coated.
3.  **Prepare the Rack:** Place a wire cooling rack over a foil-lined baking sheet. Elevating the wings allows hot air to circulate all around the meat, ensuring 360-degree crispiness.
4.  **Bake:** Place wings on the rack in a single layer. Bake at 425°F (220°C) for 45–50 minutes, flipping halfway through. They should look deeply golden brown.
5.  **Toss and Serve:** Once the wings are finished, remove them from the oven and let them rest for 2 minutes. Toss them immediately in your favorite sauce.

Serve these with a side of cold celery sticks and blue cheese or ranch dressing. You'll never go back to frying again!
